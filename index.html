AI Ping-Pong







Instructions

  1. First, keep your screen straight.
  2. Move yourself around 3-4 feet (91-122 cm) away from the screen
  3. Move your right wrist up and down - when a red dot appears on it, then the calibration is complete
  4. Now, click "Play" and move back to the far-away position you were in before.
  5. Remember that the red line is your paddle, which will be controlled by your right wrist's up-and-down motions.
  6. Happy playing!
